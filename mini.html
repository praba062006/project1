Market Purpose:

Supermarket content typically includes a wide variety of food items, household goods, and sometimes personal care products. It also includes services like pharmacies, banks, and gas stations. Supermarkets cater to diverse needs by offering groceries, fresh produce, dairy, meat, baked goods, and packaged goods.

Food Items:

  1. Fresh Produce: Fruits and vegetables
  2. Dairy: Milk, cheese, yogurt, and other dairy products
  3. Meat and Fish: Various cuts of meat, poultry, and seafood
  4. Bakery: Bread, pastries, and baked goods

Non-Food Items:

  1. Household Goods: Cleaning supplies, paper towels, etc.
  2. Personal Care: Shampoo, soap, deodorant, etc.
  3. Pharmacy: Medicines and healthcare products
  4. Pet Supplies: Food, toys, and pet care items
  5. Others: Clothing, seasonal items, electronics, etc.
